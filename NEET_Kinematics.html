Quick Reference

⚡ Equations of Motion (1D)

Velocity
v = u + at
Displacement
s = ut + ½at²
Velocity-Displacement
v² = u² + 2as
nth second
s_n = u + a(2n-1)/2

🚀 Projectile Formulas

Time of Flight
T = 2u sinθ / g
Max Height
H = u² sin²θ / 2g
Range
R = u² sin2θ / g
Max Range (θ=45°)
R_max = u² / g

🍎 Free Fall / Vertical Motion

Height fallen
h = ½gt²
Speed after falling h
v = √(2gh)
Time to fall height h
t = √(2h/g)
Max height (thrown up)
H = u² / 2g
Time to top
t = u / g

📊 Graph Interpretation

Slope of s-t graph
= velocity (v)
Slope of v-t graph
= acceleration (a)
Area under v-t graph
= displacement (s)
Area under a-t graph
= change in velocity

🔄 Relative Velocity

Same direction
v_rel = v_A - v_B
Opposite direction
v_rel = v_A + v_B
At angle θ (magnitude)
|v_rel| = √(v_A²+v_B²-2v_Av_B cosθ)

🏔 Horizontal Projectile

Horizontal motion
x = u·t (constant u)
Vertical motion
y = ½gt² (u_y = 0)
Time to hit ground
t = √(2H/g)
Horizontal range
R = u·√(2H/g)
