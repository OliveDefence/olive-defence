I Can Say!
These are the sentences you can use every single day — at school, at home, with friends. Learn them, practise them, and use them! The more you use English, the better you get!
⭐ Teacher's Tip

The best way to learn is to use these sentences every day. Print them out, stick them on the wall, and read them every morning. By the end of the week, you will say them without thinking!

In My Classroom 🏫
🙋
May I come in, please?
When you arrive late or enter the classroom
🚻
May I go to the toilet, please?
When you need to use the bathroom
💧
May I drink water, please?
When you are thirsty in class
✋
I don't understand. Can you say it again?
When the teacher explains something confusing
📝
I have finished, teacher!
When you complete your work
❓
I have a question.
When you want to ask something
✏️
Can I borrow a pencil, please?
When you need a pencil or pen
📖
Open your books to page ___.
The teacher says this — learn to listen for it!
Talking to Friends 👫
👋
What is your name?
When you meet someone new
Reply: My name is [your name].
🏠
Where do you live?
When you want to know where a friend lives
Reply: I live in [your place/city].
🎂
How old are you?
When you want to know someone's age
Reply: I am [number] years old.
🎮
Let's play together!
When you want to play with someone
Reply: Yes, let's play! / OK!
🙏
Can you help me, please?
When you need help from a friend
Reply: Sure! / Of course!
😊
You are my best friend!
Tell your best friend how special they are
Reply: You are my best friend too! 💛
Asking About Things 🔍
🤔
What is this?
When you see something you don't know
Reply: This is a [name of thing].
🎨
What colour is it?
When you want to know the colour
Reply: It is [colour].
📍
Where is the [thing]?
When you are looking for something
Reply: It is on the table / in the bag.
🔢
How many are there?
When you want to count something
Reply: There are [number] [things].
When I Don't Feel Well 🤒

🤕

💧

When you need water
😴
I am very tired today.
When you need rest
