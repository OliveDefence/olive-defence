Olive Defence Entrance Academy

About Us

Olive Defence Entrance Academy is an online learning platform dedicated to helping students prepare for competitive defence examinations such as CDS, NDA, AFCAT, and other similar exams.

Our mission is to simplify complex concepts and provide structured preparation through clear lessons, exam-oriented practice questions, and focused revision material.

Our Training Approach

At Olive Defence Entrance Academy, we focus on conceptual understanding and smart exam strategies. Our lessons are designed to help students:

  • Understand concepts quickly
  • Practice exam-level MCQs
  • Improve speed and accuracy
  • Revise important topics efficiently

The learning system includes theory lessons, quick revision sections, and quiz practice to help students strengthen their preparation.

Trainer
